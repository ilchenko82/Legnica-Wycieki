Water Installations

Detection wycieków z rur

We use acoustic methods and tracer gas to precisely pinpoint the location of damage to water and heating pipes hidden in walls and floors. This avoids unnecessary demolition of the entire bathroom or kitchen.

Our specialists have certified equipment for detecting leaks in copper, PEX, PP and steel installations.

  • Cold and hot water installations (Z.W. / C.W.U.)
  • Sewage risers and drain pipes
  • Central heating installations (C.O.)
Thermal Imaging

Underfloor Heating

Underfloor heating failures are particularly difficult to locate without specialized equipment. We use high-resolution thermal imaging cameras to "see" the path of the pipes under the floor and identify a pinpoint leak.

This method allows precise identification of the fault location, limiting repair to replacing just one tile or a small fragment of panels.

Waterproofing

Roofs and Terraces

Leaks on flat roofs and terraces often appear in a completely different place than the source of the problem. We use smoke and moisture methods to find leaks in waterproofing.

A thorough analysis helps avoid the costly replacement of the entire roof covering, focusing only on the leaking point.
