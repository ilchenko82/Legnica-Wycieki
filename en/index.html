Professional Equipment

We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment

1

Highly Sensitive Geophone

Professional electronic geophone Trubolab A-10T3 — detects even the smallest leaks through concrete, plaster, and soil up to several meters deep.

2

Thermal Camera & Moisture Meter

Detecting temperature differences and measuring moisture levels to locate leaks behind walls and in floors.

3

Inspection Endoscopes

Industrial cameras for inspecting pipes, enclosures, and hard-to-reach areas. We have several different cameras for this purpose. HD imaging.

4

Tracer Gas & Sensor

We introduce a safe H2/N2 mixture, which we then detect with a specialized, highly sensitive gas detector upon release.

Contact
