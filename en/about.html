About Us

Professional water leak detection with passion and experience

Who We Are

WyciekiPro is a modern company specializing in non-invasive water leak detection. Although the brand was established in 2026, it is formed by experts with over 10 years of experience in the plumbing, heating, and installation industry. Our team has gained its high qualifications in leak localization through years of work on complex projects in renowned companies in the technical sector. We provide full professionalism and nationwide coverage, and our headquarters is located in Warsaw.

Our Specialization

  • Non-invasive water pipe leak detection (hot and cold water)
  • Underfloor heating and central heating diagnostics
  • Sewage inspection with endoscopic camera
  • Waterproofing integrity testing for roofs and terraces
  • Professional drying after floods (in cooperation with partners)

Our Equipment

We work exclusively with professional diagnostic equipment: acoustic geophones, high-resolution thermal imaging cameras, tracer gas detectors (hydrogen + nitrogen), inspection endoscopes, and test pressure gauges. We carry all necessary equipment in a single vehicle — which allows us to locate over 95% of faults during the first visit.
